BIOL 8: Fundamentals of Biology

General Information

BIOL 8: Fundamentals of Biology. 2 lec., 2 lab. hr.; 3 cr. A survey course in biology designed for students in Adult Collegiate Education (ACE) and the LEAP project. The areas of cell biology, heredity, development, physiology, ecology, and evolution are covered. Emphasis will be placed on human-related topics such as health, the environment, and current developments in biology. No previous knowledge of biology or chemistry is assumed. Not open to students who have taken BIOL 11. Fall, Spring
